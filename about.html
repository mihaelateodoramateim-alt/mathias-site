Our Story

Founded in 2022 in Romania, Mathias IT Solutions was born from the vision of a young engineer passionate about technology and innovation since childhood. Competing in national math olympiads and excelling in personal and academic projects, our founder became an engineer in Electronics, IT, and Automation. Today, Mathias IT strives for excellence in IT services, outsourcing, and support.
