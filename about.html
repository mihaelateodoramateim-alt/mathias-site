Who we are

Engineering mindset. People first.

Founded in 2022 in Romania, Mathias IT Solutions blends the discipline of engineering with a practical approach to people and products.

Our Founder

Our founder is an engineer in Electronics, Information Technology and Automation. From an early age he dismantled and rebuilt devices, wrote code for fun, and tested ideas in real-life projects. He competed in numerous competitions and became a national mathematics olympiad laureate. That drive for excellence became the company’s north star.

As the company grew, we kept a simple promise: do the simple things exceptionally well—then scale. We prefer clarity over buzzwords, and results over slides. We’ve learned that steady progress beats sporadic sprints.
